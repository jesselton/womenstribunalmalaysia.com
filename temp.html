Issues

Thematic areas and their respective issues will be drawn from Malaysia’s state obligations to international human rights treaties and global commitments and, more importantly, the gaps in their implementation. These include, amongst others, the Convention on the Elimination of All Forms of Discrimination against Women (CEDAW); Convention on the Rights of the Child (CRC); Convention on the Rights of Persons with Disabilities (CRPD); Beijing Platform for Action; SDG 2030; Universal Periodic Review; International Conference on Population and Development; and related  ILO conventions.
